How It Works

Three steps from weathered to warm again.

01

Assess & Quote

We look at the piece (or photos), talk through condition and goals, and send a real, scope-based quote.

02

Clean

Deep, hand-scrubbed clean to lift years of gray, mildew, and grime without damaging the wood or wicker underneath. Sanding is added on top of this step only for pieces that actually need it.

03

Protect

A premium protective coat — Gold Care, Summer Classics, or another trusted product suited to your piece — locks in the finish against sun and moisture, extending the life of the restoration.
