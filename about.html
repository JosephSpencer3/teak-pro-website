About Teak Pro

Furniture this good shouldn’t end up at the curb.

Teak Pro started with a simple frustration: watching perfectly good outdoor furniture get replaced instead of restored, because nobody made restoration feel careful and precise enough to trust with a piece worth keeping.

We’re a small, hands-on operation — not a franchise, not a call center. Every piece that comes through gets worked by people who actually care whether it turns out right, because our name is on it. We serve homeowners and partners throughout the Birmingham area, including Mountain Brook, Vestavia Hills, Homewood, and surrounding communities.

“Replacing a $4,000 dining set because it’s gray isn’t maintenance — it’s waste. Restoring it to new or near-new condition costs a fraction of replacing it, even though our work is never the cheapest option out there.”
What We Believe

The principles behind every project.

Honest Assessments

If a piece just needs a deep clean and a fresh protector application, that’s what we quote. We don’t upsell work a piece doesn’t need.

Done Right, Not Fast

Hand work takes time. We’d rather deliver a finish that lasts seasons than rush a job that fades in months.

Respect for the Piece

Real teak and well-made wicker are worth preserving. We treat every project like it’s the customer’s favorite piece — because it usually is.

Who We Work With

Homeowners, designers, and retailers across Birmingham.

Whether it’s a single family patio set or an ongoing pipeline from a design consultant or furniture retailer, we bring the same standard to every project.
